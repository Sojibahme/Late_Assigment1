The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy,
digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to
enhance connectivity and access to services for
all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure
a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support
economic growth and global competitiveness.
